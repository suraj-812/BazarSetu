Security & Compliance

Last Updated: October 2026

BazarSetu prioritizes the security of your data, financial information, and personal identity. We employ enterprise-grade security protocols to ensure that our marketplace remains safe for Customers, Vendors, and Delivery Partners.

1. Payment Security

All transactions on BazarSetu are fully encrypted. We are PCI-DSS compliant, meaning your payment credentials are tokenized and processed securely through leading payment gateways via 256-bit SSL encryption. We never store your full credit card number or CVV.

2. Infrastructure and Data Protection

Our platform is hosted on highly secure, compliant cloud infrastructure providers. We implement strict operational safeguards, including:

  • Regular penetration testing and vulnerability scanning.
  • End-to-end encryption for in-transit data (TLS 1.3).
  • Granular role-based access control (RBAC) to ensure employees only access data critical to their duties.

3. Account Protection

We actively monitor our platform for suspicious activities. Users are encouraged to use strong passwords and enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) if available. If we detect unusual logins, your account may be temporarily restricted to prevent unauthorized use.

4. Reporting Security Vulnerabilities

If you are a security researcher and have discovered a vulnerability, please report it immediately. BazarSetu operates a responsible disclosure program. Submit findings confidentially to surajmishra220704@gmail.com.

5. Phishing and Fraud

BazarSetu representatives will never ask for your password, OTP, or CVV over the phone or via email. Always ensure you are on the official domain before entering any credentials.
