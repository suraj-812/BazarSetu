Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2026

At BazarSetu, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, phone number, and delivery address when you create an account or place an order.
  • Transaction Data: Details of orders placed, payment methods used (we do not store full credit card details), and transaction history.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect precise location data to show you nearby vendors and track deliveries.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers for analytics and security purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including:

  • Processing transactions and sending order confirmations.
  • Facilitating the delivery of products by sharing necessary details with delivery partners.
  • Personalizing your experience and showing relevant nearby shops and offers.
  • Providing customer support and analyzing platform usage to improve our App.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information with:

  • Vendors: Only the information necessary to fulfill your order (e.g., items ordered).
  • Delivery Partners: Name, phone number, and delivery address to ensure successful drop-offs.
  • Service Providers: Third-party companies that assist us with operations, such as payment processing and secure data hosting.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

BazarSetu uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to analyze trends, administer the website, track users' movements around the platform, and gather demographic information. You can control the use of cookies at the individual browser level.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures (such as SSL encryption) to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can manage your account settings directly in the app or contact our support team for assistance.

7. Contact Us
